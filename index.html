A Little About Me

My first foray into web development was using Wetpaint to create a website about my favorite animal (circa 2008). A couple of years later, I took a course in high school on web design, in which I learned the basics of HTML and styling. Fast forward a few years and my interest in web development reignited.

I’ve learned a lot from courses like Beginning Modern Javascript, The Web Developer Bootcamp, and Modern React with Redux. I’ve solidified my understanding of different technologies and concepts through numerous projects.

I enjoy making things that look good, work well, and are easy to use. What I love most about web development is that there is always something new to learn.
